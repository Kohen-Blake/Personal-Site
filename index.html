I’m a first-year Computer Science student at the University of Alabama with a concentration in cybersecurity. I enjoy turning ideas into secure, working software that people can easily use.

I’m an active member of Crimson Defense (UA’s cybersecurity club), where I practice both defense and offense in sanctioned environments. I also keep a homelab for networking experiments and deployment practice, and I ship real-world tools while documenting what I learn.

When I’m not behind a keyboard, you’ll find me on my motorcycle, practicing my saxophone, or in a game session with friends. If I sound like someone that you would like to work with please feel free to contact me.
